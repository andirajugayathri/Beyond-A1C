Clinical Practice Guidelines

Risk-Based Management Protocols

⚠️ High Risk (Score >75th percentile)
▲
✓ Intensify glucose management (HbA1c target <7.0%)
✓ Initiate cardioprotective therapies (ACE/ARB, statins)
✓ Quarterly monitoring with ophthalmology/nephrology referrals
✓ Consider CGM and advanced diabetes technologies
⊜ Moderate Risk (25th-75th percentile)
▼
✓ Standard glucose management (HbA1c target <7.5%)
✓ Biannual comprehensive screening
✓ Lifestyle modification support and education
⊖ Low Risk (<25th percentile)
▼
✓ Maintain current management approach
✓ Annual comprehensive screening
✓ Continue preventive care and monitoring
