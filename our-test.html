Test Overview

beyondA1C™ Comprehensive Diabetes Risk Assessment

Methodology: Multiplexed immunoassay with algorithmic analysis
Intended Use: Aid in diabetes complication risk assessment for adults with type 2 diabetes
Sample Type: Human serum
Clinical Indication: 5-year risk prediction for retinopathy, nephropathy, neuropathy, and cardiovascular complications

Simple Ordering, Seamless Integration

Streamlined workflow designed for clinical efficiency

How to Order

1.

Account Setup

Register your facility through our online portal. Complete CLIA verification and billing setup. Access ordering forms and clinical resources

2.

Test Ordering

Order online, by phone, or through EHR integration. Standard requisition forms available for download. Batch ordering capabilities for high-volume practices

3.

Sample Collection

Standard venipuncture (5ml serum, fasting preferred). Compatible with routine lab workflows. No special handling or processing required

Sample Requirements

  • Sample Type: Serum (5ml minimum)
  • Collection Tubes: SST or red-top tubes acceptable
  • Storage: Refrigerate 2-8°C if not shipped within 24 hours
  • Shipping: Ambient temperature, Monday-Thursday shipments preferred
  • Stability: 5 days refrigerated, 2 days ambient

Logistics & Shipping

  • Collection Kits: Prepaid tubes and requisitions
  • Shipping: Ambient or refrigerated
  • Shipping: Real-time shipment tracking via online porta
  • Emergency Processing: STAT orders available with 24-hour turnaround

beyondA1C™ Technical Specifications

Technical Performance

Advanced Analysis

Precision (Repeatability): CV <5% within-run, <8% between-run

Accuracy: 98.5% concordance with reference methods

Linearity: R² >0.99 across clinical range

Detection Limit: Varies by biomarker (0.1-10 pg/mL range)

Analytical Range: Covers 99.5% of clinical specimens

Clinical Performance

Sensitivity: 89% for high-risk complication prediction

Specificity: 94% for low-risk identification

Positive Predictive Value: 87% in high-prevalence populations

Negative Predictive Value: 95% for complication-free outcomes

Area Under Curve (AUC): 0.92 for composite complication endpoint

Preferred but not required
